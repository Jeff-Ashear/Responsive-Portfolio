E!ntanglements





E!ntanglements is a work written specifiaclly for the musicians shown performing it in the above video. Richard vonForester (cello), Brian Ebert (base clarinet), and Reggie Berg (piano), were so enjoyable to watch that I wanted to write something to their particular strengths. More specifically, they all have the ability to navigate the ranges of their instruments with uncanny speed and precision. There is a speaker hidden inside the piano which plays back some audio, which was captured durring the rehearsals of this piece and later aranged and manipulated by the composer. The intention is that the electronic elements will be a surprise to the audience.
