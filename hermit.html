The Hermit and the Exile





The Hermit and the Exile is a work for solo soprano, deidcated to Emily Stephenson. It was performed here by Megan Buness with the Playground Ensembel. The piece has minimal program notation to encourage the listener to form their own impressions. Ideally, the notes should contain only the following quote by Joseph Roux:


"Solitude vivifies, isolation kills."
